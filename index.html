Origins

The Thach Thuc Academic Competition was officially launched by the Faculty of Information Technology – University of Science, Vietnam National University, Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM) in 2001. It is held annually from early March to mid-April, attracting over 800 students from universities and colleges related to the field of Information Technology in Ho Chi Minh City.

Over the years, through continuous development and innovation, the Thach Thuc Academic Competition has become an inseparable tradition for faculty members and students studying and teaching Information Technology at the University of Science, VNU-HCM, as well as other universities in Ho Chi Minh City.
